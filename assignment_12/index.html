The University of Montana Grizzlies football team, commonly known as the UM Griz, is a proud and storied collegiate football program that commands both respect and enthusiasm. Representing the University of Montana in Missoula, the Grizzlies have established themselves as a force within the NCAA's Football Championship Subdivision (FCS). Known for their competitive spirit and dedicated fan base, the UM Griz football team has a rich history of success, marked by memorable victories and a tradition of producing top-tier athletes. The team's home games at Washington-Grizzly Stadium are a spectacle, with roaring crowds clad in maroon and silver passionately supporting their beloved Grizzlies. Beyond the field, the UM Griz fosters a sense of community, bringing together students, alumni, and fans alike to celebrate the enduring spirit of Montana football.
